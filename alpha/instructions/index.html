What to Expect

As one of our first-ever alpha testers, you’ll:

  • 🤫 Get early access to the app before anyone else.
  • 💡 Help us shape the entire experience with your direct feedback.
  • 🌱 Be part of a small, foundational group making HumanKind better from the ground up.

We’re looking for testers who can spend a little time exploring the app, sharing your honest thoughts, and letting us know what feels great and what could be better.

Feedback Schedule

We’ll ask for feedback at a few key moments, but you can also share your thoughts anytime something comes to mind!

  • 🧠 Anytime Feedback: Use this link whenever you notice something worth sharing → Feedback Form
  • 🗓️ September 6: Alpha Onboarding Process Starts, invite anyone you want to join you on the app by sending them this link → Invite Form
  • 🗓️ September 13: Alpha Onboarding Process Ends, we won’t be accepting new users after this point
  • 🗓️ September 20: First thoughts and impressions of HumanKind.
  • 🗓️ October 18: Reflections after one month with the app.
  • 🏁 November 30: Alpha testing wraps up (but your access to the app continues!)

Getting Started: NDA & App Access

Since you’ll be seeing a very early version of HumanKind, we ask all testers to agree to a simple non-disclosure agreement (NDA). This just means please don’t share screenshots, features, or details of the app outside our private testing group.

  1. You will receive a link in your email with directions to sign the NDA.
  2. Once we have your signature, we will add you to the test group in the app store.
  3. You will then get an email from TestFlight that will guide you through the setup process.
