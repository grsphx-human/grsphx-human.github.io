HumanKind Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how HumanKind App (Crooner Holdings LLC, "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use the HumanKind mobile application (the "App").

1. The Short Version

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect the information necessary to provide the core service of sending and tracking gratitude with your friends. Your private messages of gratitude are never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Information We Collect

Data Type Details & Purpose Linked to Identity? Voluntarily Provided?
Email Address Used for account creation, login authentication, and password recovery. Yes Yes
User ID (Identifier) An internal unique identifier used to manage your account, friends list, streaks, and leaderboard data. Yes Not Applicable
Messages (Gratitudes) The content of the messages you send and receive — core function of the App. Yes Yes
Friends List Users you’ve designated as friends. Required to send messages. Yes Yes
Usage Data Tracks interactions such as messages sent and login streaks for leaderboards. Yes Not Applicable
Diagnostics (Crash Logs) Anonymized data used only for debugging and technical issue resolution. No Not Applicable

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide App Functionality: Account authentication, sending messages, displaying message archive, and managing your friends list.
  • To Run Leaderboards and Streaks: Calculating streaks and leaderboard placement.
  • Security and Safety: Monitoring and preventing fraudulent or illegal activity.
  • Maintenance: Diagnosing and fixing crashes or bugs.

4. Data Security and Storage

We prioritize your data security and use strict internal access controls to limit employee access.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information or gratitude messages. We only share data under the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: For example, if you choose to share a streak image outside the App.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or legal process.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and other providers who must follow strict confidentiality and security standards.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your information within a reasonable timeframe unless law requires otherwise.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Most settings are available in-app. For help, contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be communicated via this page or in-app notifications.
